About

At SPIN Global, we are working with private and public entities to make generational investments in community resilience measured by their social impact, economic impact, and public benefits. The SPIN Global Community Disaster Resilience Fund exists to mobilize private capital towards public benefit projects that significantly reduce exposure to threats and hazards while increasing economic opportunities, especially for underserved and historically marginalized populations.
